## How to Become a Contributor

1. **Start Small**: Look for issues labeled `good first issue` or `help wanted`
2. **Read Guidelines**: Check our [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md)
3. **Join Discussions**: Participate in GitHub Discussions and issues
4. **Be Patient**: Code review takes time, but we appreciate your contributions
5. **Stay Engaged**: Regular contributors often become core team members
